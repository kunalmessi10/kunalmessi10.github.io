Kunal Pratap Singh

I am currently a research assistant at the Computer Vision Lab at GIST working with Prof. Jonghyun Choi, where I work on computer vision and machine learning.

I am interested in building embodied agents that can explore and continuously reason about their environments in a self-supervised manner. Specifically, I'm interested in Perception For Robotics, Active Visual Learning, Lifelong Learning and Efficient Neural Networks.

I've worked on Binary Architecture Search and Embodied Instruction Following. I did my bachelors at the Indian Institute of Roorkee.
